CityCraft


CityCraft is a game designed to bring young urban students, living in low income areas, into STEM education, and hopefully on the path to a STEM career. A prominent point in our research was that current STEM education is often exclusive to those who have the funds to pay for the extra education or materials, so we designed something all inclusive for students. It combines role-playing and active learning through experience to introduce the students to jobs such as civil engineering and urban planning. See the manual here!
