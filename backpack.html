RPI Backpack


Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) offers a backpack with their mobile computer package, but it doesn't properly match the items it carries and as a one-size-fits-all, doesn't fit half the students that have it. An incorrectly sized backpack it uncomfortable and does not distribute weight properly if the wearer does not fit the size standards. The design of the backpack was also overcomplicated, so we decided to research and propose a better solution.
