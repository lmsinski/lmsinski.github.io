Eddy VNA Focus Group


My team was assigned to work with Eddy VNA to improve the quality of their work system and make their lives easier. The biggest problem for Eddy VNA visiting nurses and other staff was the lack of communication between their coworkers. In a career that requires the most up-to-date information, they were behind in technology. My group designed a simple interface that would allow them to keep up with updates on patients and send quick notes and schedule changes to each other. We gained experience working in focus groups and having discussions with our clients, research, and conceptualization.
